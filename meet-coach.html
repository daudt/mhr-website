Meet the Coach

Coach Doug Croft

Welcome to Mile High Runners!

My name is Doug Croft, and I'm the founder and head coach of Mile High Runners. With years of experience in distance running and coaching, I'm passionate about helping runners of all levels achieve their goals.

Whether you're training for your first 5K or chasing a Boston Qualifier, I believe in creating a supportive, inclusive environment where every runner can thrive. Our training programs are designed to help you improve while staying healthy and enjoying the journey.

I've had the privilege of coaching hundreds of runners in the Erie, Colorado area, and I've seen firsthand how group training can transform not just your running, but your entire outlook on fitness and health.

My Philosophy:

  • Training should be challenging but enjoyable
  • Every runner deserves personalized attention
  • Community and friendship are just as important as fitness
  • Injury prevention is key to long-term success

I look forward to meeting you and helping you reach your running goals. Come join us for a workout and experience the Mile High Runners difference!
